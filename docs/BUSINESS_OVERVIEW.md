# Intemso — Business Overview

> **The gig marketplace built for Ghanaian university students.**

---

## What Is Intemso?

Intemso is an online marketplace that connects **university students in Ghana** with **people and businesses who need tasks done**. Think of it as the Upwork for campus — but designed from scratch for the Ghanaian student experience.

Students can earn flexible income around their lecture schedule by picking up short-term gigs — anything from delivering food on campus to designing a logo for a local business. Employers get access to a pool of verified, hungry, talented young people who are ready to work.

**Our mission:** Empower university students to earn while they learn, and give businesses a reliable, on-demand talent pool they can trust.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

**For students:**
- Most students need extra income but can't commit to a 9-to-5 job
- Traditional freelancing platforms (Upwork, Fiverr) have high barriers — you need a portfolio, reviews, years of experience just to get started
- There's no organized way for students to find short-term, flexible work that fits around lectures and exams

**For employers:**
- Small businesses, entrepreneurs, and campus organizations regularly need help with tasks but don't want to hire full-time staff
- Finding reliable help is disorganized — WhatsApp groups, word-of-mouth, prayers
- No way to verify who you're hiring or guarantee the work will get done

**Intemso fixes both sides.** Students sign up in 2 minutes with just their university email — no CV, no portfolio, no experience required. Employers post what they need, students apply, and the platform handles everything: matching, communication, payments, and protection for both sides.

## How It Works

### For Students

**1. Sign up (2 minutes)**
Create an account with your university email and verify it. Add your name, university, skills, and what kind of work you're interested in. That's it — you're in.

**2. Browse and apply**
Search gigs by category, budget, location, or urgency. When you find one you like, submit a proposal — your pitch to the employer explaining why you're the right person. Include your proposed rate and timeline.

**3. Get hired and work**
The employer reviews proposals and picks who they want. You'll chat directly through the app to discuss details. For bigger projects, the work is broken into checkpoints (milestones) so everyone stays on the same page.

**4. Get paid securely**
When you complete work and the employer approves it, the money goes straight to your bank account or mobile money. The platform holds the money in escrow the entire time — so you're guaranteed to get paid for work you complete, and the employer only pays for work they approve.

### For Employers

**1. Post your gig (free)**
Describe what you need, set your budget and timeline. You can either set a fixed price for the whole job or pay by the hour.

**2. Review applications**
Students apply with proposals. You can see their profiles, ratings from past work, skills, and university. Chat with them before deciding.

**3. Track the work**
For bigger projects, you fund milestones upfront (the money is held safely by the platform, not released yet). The student works, submits deliverables, and you review.

**4. Approve and pay**
Happy with the work? Approve it and the payment is released to the student. Not happy? Request a revision (up to 2 rounds per milestone). Leave a rating and review so the next employer knows what to expect.

## How We Make Money

### Students pay a small service fee on earnings (sliding scale):

| Earnings with a client | Platform fee |
|---|---|
| First GH₵500 | 15% |
| GH₵501 – GH₵2,000 | 10% |
| Above GH₵2,000 | 5% |

The fee **decreases** the more a student earns with the same client. This rewards long-term relationships — the more you work with someone, the more you keep.

**Real example:** A student earns GH₵3,000 total with one client:
- GH₵500 × 15% = GH₵75
- GH₵1,500 × 10% = GH₵150
- GH₵1,000 × 5% = GH₵50
- **Total fee: GH₵275 out of GH₵3,000 (9.2% overall)**

### Students also use "Connects" to apply for gigs:

- Every student gets **15 free connects per month**
- Each proposal costs 2–6 connects depending on the gig
- **Earn more through activity:** complete gigs (+5), get 5-star reviews (+3), leave reviews (+1), complete your profile (+10), log in daily (+1, max 5/week)
- **Or buy packs if you prefer:**
  - 10 connects = GH₵5
  - 20 connects = GH₵9
  - 40 connects = GH₵16

**Why connects?** They stop students from spamming every gig with low-effort applications. If you have to "spend" something to apply, you only apply to gigs you actually want and can do. Most students will never need to buy connects — active platform usage earns more than enough.

### Employers pay nothing to the platform.
Posting gigs, browsing talent, hiring — all free. They only pay the agreed budget to the student.

## What Makes It Safe & Trustworthy

### 1. Verified Students Only
Every student must verify through their university email. No random accounts — you know you're dealing with a real university student.

### 2. Escrow-Protected Payments
When an employer funds a milestone, the money is held securely by our payment partner (Paystack — one of Africa's most trusted payment processors). The student sees the money is there and works with confidence. The employer knows the money won't be released until they approve the work. If the employer doesn't respond within 14 days, the money auto-releases to the student.

### 3. Ratings & Reviews (Both Ways)
After every completed gig, both sides leave ratings and reviews (1–5 stars plus written feedback). These are public on their profiles. Good work builds reputation. Bad behavior is visible to everyone.

### 4. Job Success Score
Every student gets a score (0–100%) calculated from their track record — client ratings, completion rate, responsiveness, and disputes. This score is visible to employers and helps them hire with confidence.

### 5. Talent Badges
Students earn visible badges as they build their reputation:
- **Rising Talent** — Completed at least 1 gig with a perfect score
- **Top Rated** — 90%+ success score, 10+ completed gigs, strong earnings history
- **Top Rated Plus** — By invitation only. The best of the best.

### 6. Dispute Resolution
If something goes wrong, either side can open a dispute. Our team mediates and resolves it fairly within 48 hours. No one gets scammed.

### 7. Content Moderation
All gig listings, messages, and community posts are monitored. Users can report inappropriate content, and our admin team takes action — removing posts, warning users, or suspending accounts when necessary.
